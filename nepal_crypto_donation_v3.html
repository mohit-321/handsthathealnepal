▸ The Cause

In the rural lowlands of Morang District, Province No. 1, hundreds of elderly men and women lie bedridden — not because medicine doesn't exist, but because no one can reach them. Disabled children grow up without wheelchairs. Grandmothers with wounds go unbandaged for weeks.

This campaign is run by a single anonymous individual who grew up watching this happen. No organisation. No overhead. Every fraction of crypto received goes directly to mobility aids, monthly care visits, hygiene kits, and nutrition for those who have been made invisible by poverty.
